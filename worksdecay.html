Decay

Decay

Everything around us is either growing or decaying. Whether it be physical objects or the human mind, everything is changing daily. This open ended project was edited using Adobe Photoshop and Camera Raw in Adobe Bridge. The images shown are of the Athens Lunatic Asylum in April 2022. The Asylum was established in 1874 and closed its doors in 1993. Renovations began in 2001, after being abandoned for a few years. When building the Ridges, “Doctors hoped that the beautiful surroundings would assist patients in recovering their mental health.” Even after the renovations, the building is over 100 years old and decaying by the day.

Many of the patients hospitalized within the Ridges suffered from different mental illnesses. I wanted to capture not only the decay of the building but of the patients inside. Many did not get to leave the hospital and are buried in the cemetery next door. My overall goal was for people to see that everything around us is decaying. Natual elements, artificial elements, us as living beings, and more are all decaying by the second.
