About Me!

Hello! My name is Audrey Seymour and I’m currently a third year Graphic Design student at Ohio University. I also am minoring in Marketing and Studio Art. I was drawn to Graphic Design because of my passion for drawing and expressing creativity since I was little. I am especially interested in using design to help with different marketing strategies. I am also interested in designing for different name brands. Outside of school, you can find me hiking, swimming, or going to concerts! I can’t wait to learn more skills and see what OU brings me!
